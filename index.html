Introduction

Bitterkat was born and raised in Utah. She got married in Taiwan (it's a funny story), and she and her husband of 28 years have raised 2 children (now adults) in Seattle.

Bitterkat has a B.A. from the University of Washington (Chinese major) and has a career in technology (it just kind of happened).

Bitterkat spends the better part of her free time beer tasting, trying out new recipes (vegetarian cooking is her latest endeavor), and marathon training.
